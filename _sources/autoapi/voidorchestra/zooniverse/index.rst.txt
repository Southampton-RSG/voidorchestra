voidorchestra.zooniverse
========================

.. py:module:: voidorchestra.zooniverse

.. autoapi-nested-parse::

   The MoleMarshal package is a collection of packages to automate
   the creation, uploading and management of subject sets to the MoleMarshal
   Zooniverse project.

   The `zooniverse` sub-package is responsible for management of the
   Zooniverse infrastructure including adding to the MoleMarshal database,
   creating/uploading subjects and subject sets and matching classifications from
   users to stamps.
